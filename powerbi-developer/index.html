FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before you hire a Power BI developer.

How quickly can a Power BI developer start on my project?+
In most cases, we can match you with a qualified developer and get them onboarded within 48 hours of your initial consultation. For enterprise engagements, we typically schedule a 3–5 day ramp period.
Do your developers hold Microsoft certifications?+
Yes — all our Power BI developers hold current Microsoft certifications including PL-300 (Power BI Data Analyst) and relevant Azure data certifications. We verify credentials before onboarding every developer.
What data sources can your developers connect to Power BI?+
Our developers have experience connecting Power BI to hundreds of sources: SQL Server, Azure SQL, PostgreSQL, MySQL, Salesforce, Google Analytics, SharePoint, REST APIs, SAP, Oracle, Dynamics 365, and flat files.
Can I hire a Power BI developer on a part-time basis?+
Absolutely. We offer part-time (20 hrs/week), full-time (40 hrs/week), hourly on-demand, and fixed-scope project engagements. You only pay for what you need.
Is my company's data safe working with your developers?+
Data security is paramount. All engagements begin with a signed NDA. Our developers follow strict data handling protocols and can work within your existing security infrastructure including VPNs and on-premise connections.
What if I'm not satisfied with the work delivered?+
We offer unlimited revisions on Professional and Enterprise plans. For Starter projects, one revision round is included. If you're unhappy with a developer, we'll replace them at no cost within the first 2 weeks.
